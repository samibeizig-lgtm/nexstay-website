About
Nexstay

The story of a passion for hospitality — and an ambition: to make every property a memorable experience.

Our Story
"We believe every property deserves to be a memorable experience"

Founded in 2024, Nexstay is a Tunisian company specialising in professional concierge services for short-term rentals. We take care of the operational side of your rental through on-the-ground expertise and a premium approach.

Nexstay has grown to cover the most attractive areas of Tunisia — Greater Tunis (La Marsa, Gammarth, Ain Zaghouan, Lac, Soukra, Menzah) and Hammamet.

Every property owner who trusts us benefits from complete, transparent and optimized concierge service. Our Essential and Premium packages adapt to your needs, with no hidden fees.

Who We Are

Our Mission

To transform every property into an unforgettable experience for guests, while maximizing profitability for our owners. We believe quality of service is the best business strategy.

"Quality is never an accident. It is always the result of intelligent effort."

Our Vision

To become the reference for professional concierge services in Tunisia, combining technology, field expertise and exceptional human service. Progressive expansion into the main tourist cities.
