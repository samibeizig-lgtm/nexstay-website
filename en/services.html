Our approach

Complete expertise at the service of your property

Nexstay takes care of the logistics for your property on rental platforms. From assistance creating your listing to handling check-outs, including guest welcome, professional cleaning and revenue optimization advice — you keep control, we handle the operations.

Our Essential (15% all-inclusive) and Premium (25% all-inclusive) plans cover all our services, with no hidden fees or surprises. You receive your income every month, with a detailed report.
