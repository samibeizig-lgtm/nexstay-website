Living far from Tunisia?
Your property deserves a local presence.

Thousands of TRE own an apartment or villa in Tunisia that they cannot maintain remotely. Nexstay handles guest welcome, cleaning, maintenance and monitoring of your property — keeping you at ease wherever you are.

Guest Welcome & Key Handover

Our team is present at every arrival and departure on your behalf, 7 days a week. Your guests are welcomed and assisted — without you having to travel.

Cleaning & Restoration

Between each stay, professional cleaning, fresh linen and a thorough inspection of the property. Your property is always in impeccable condition.

Photos & Condition Report

Systematic photos after every visit, regular condition reports. You know exactly what state your apartment is in, without needing to travel.

Maintenance & Repairs

Plumbing, electrical, minor everyday repairs: we coordinate local tradespeople on your behalf. You approve decisions, we handle execution.
